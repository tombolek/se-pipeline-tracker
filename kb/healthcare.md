**Proof Point:**

Baylor brought on Ataccama Data Quality & Catalog to help gain deep visibility into their data, improve the quality of the data, classify the data against business sensitivity, PHI and PII, while creating a lineage map of how that data is being used across the organization. Baylor also generates revenue off of their data but need to be in compliance with what data they can legally release without legal liability. ### CAQH (Council for Affordable Quality Healthcare)

**About:** CAQH is a non-profit alliance of health plans and related associations dedicated to streamlining the business of healthcare. They offer solutions for provider credentialing, verification, directory maintenance, coordination of benefits, and more.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Products** | DQ, MDM, AI |
| **Business Initiative(s)** |  |

**Proof Point:**

CAQH is a non-profit alliance of health plans and related associations dedicated to streamlining the business of healthcare. They offer solutions for provider credentialing, verification, directory maintenance, coordination of benefits, and more. The Nexus project is a global data management initiative for CAQH, led by Capgemini, that seeks to establish a unified source for provider data, with high levels of accuracy, completeness, and consistency. CAQH believes establishing this centralized resource will improve efficiency, particularly in provider onboarding (credentialing and enrollment), through automation and better data integration. This should in turn reduce costs, improve turnaround times, and, most importantly, pave the way for the development of revenue generating premium data services.

Poor data quality has historically limited CAQH's ability to offer valuable data-driven insights and premium services. As part of their operating model, providers, or authorized parties acting on their behalf (e.g., BCBS), enter and update their information (such as credentials, specialties, practice locations, etc.) through the CAQH portal. This helps streamline the provider enrollment and credentialing process for health plans and other healthcare organizations. It is also a tedious process, and as a result this information is often inaccurate, duplicated, and inconsistent.

To relieve these pain points, CAQH turned to Ataccama. Our full platform, including both Data Quality and Master Data Management, will enable CAQH to address its core data quality challenges by providing tools for profiling, cleansing, standardizing, enriching, and matching provider data. In particular, our Data Governance capabilities will help CAQH establish a framework for managing data quality and ensuring compliance. They will also be taking advantage of GenerativeAI features to automate their rule creation process and streamline data management.
