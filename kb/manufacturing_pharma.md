### Boehringer Ingelheim

**About:** Founded in 1885, Boehringer Ingelheim is a pharmaceutical company which concentrates on the development of human pharmaceuticals, biopharmaceuticals, chemicals, and animal health products.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Products** | DQ |
| **Business Initiative(s)** | New Product Development & R&D |

**Proof Point:**

Boehringer Ingelheim are a major pharmaceutical organization offering a wide range of products for both human and veterinary use cases. They have a vast collection of data that needs to be governed in order to meet both business and regulatory requirements. However, they did not have a robust Data Quality governance program. Incomplete data is particularly high risk when it comes to data collected from drug trials, which have an impact on their ability to ultimately gain approval for the resulting products.

Efforts to govern both their internally and externally sourced data were happening in silos across the organization, using multiple, disparate tools. Boehringer Ingelheim recognized this was not scalable. They had data that was both incomplete and not tagged with standard terms and definitions necessary to adequately manage it. As a result, they invited regulatory risk. 

Ataccama's Data Quality solution was selected to help them identify, resolve and manage their data across the organization. The initial business initiative pertained to drug trials. They leverage our business terms and detection rules to rapidly identify data types and overlay consistent definitions. They use profiling alongside data quality checks to identify duplicate and incomplete data. 

Ataccama solution has allowed Boehringer Ingelheim to locate and correct inconsistent data formats such as email address and contact details stemming from their trials. Gaps in this information can lead to serious potential issues related to the successful completion of their work. As such, Boehringer Ingelheim uses the output of our data quality checks to point researchers towards data that needs to be rectified to esnure the quality of the overall study.

### Roche

**About:** Founded in 1886, Roche is a swiss multinational healthcare company that operates in the pharmaceutical and diagnositcs industries. Roche is a public company headquartered out of Basel, Switzerland.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Products** | DQ |
| **Business Initiative(s)** | Supply Chain Optimization |

**Proof Point:**

Founded in 1886, Roche is a swiss multinational healthcare company that operates in the pharmaceutical and diagnositcs industries. Roche is a public company headquartered out of Basel, Switzerland. Manufacturing is distributed geographically for Roche, while purchasing is centralized. This fragments information on what materials are used, how they are used, and to what extent. Within Roche, there is an organizational push to ensure accurate reporting and rapid remediation of quality issues in in their supply chain to help with quicker and seamless production of medicines. This impacts both top line revenue and reduces cost by allowing for easier identification of potential economies of scale.

At the root of the problem was incorrect and inconsistent information in SAP ERP. Roche's original Data Quality program wasn't created from a business centric approach. Rather, it was an IT driven initiative. As a result, there was heavy dependency on IT for maintenance, a resource intensive and slow approach. Ataccama was selected as the platform of choice to scale the Data Quality program enterprise wide. Today, Ataccama Data Governance is used to define rules and checks on data, our Issue Tracking is being used to rectify and resolve data issues, and our Data Quality rules are applied on supply chain data residing in SAP, which then lands into Snowflake before going into reporting systems.
